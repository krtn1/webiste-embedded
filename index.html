Apa Itu Embedded question

Embedded question

Embedded question memerlukan Question Word = 1.What 2.When 3.Where 4.Why 5.Who 6.How


Dalam Kalimat atau sentencese ada 3 jenis kalimat = 1.positive 2.Negative 3.Question

Embedded Question Merupkan gabungan atara Positive/Negative Sentencese Dengan Question


Contoh = "WE haven't ascertained WHERE the meeting will take place?".

Contoh = "I have no idea HOW LONG the interview will take?".

Formula Embedded Question

Ada 2 Jenis Embedded Question

SUBJECT + VERB(PHARSE) + QUESTION WORD + SUBJECT2 + VERB

NOTE = "There MUST NOT be an auxiliary between the question word and subject2"


Tetapi Kalau Menggunakan Auxiliary/TO Be

QUESTION WORD + SUBJECT + Auxiliary/TO Be


Contoh = "WHERE the bank is?"
